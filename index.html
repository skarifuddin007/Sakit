Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TaskFlow AI

What's included in the Free plan?

The Free plan includes up to 5 projects, basic task management features, email support, and access to core functionalities perfect for individuals and small teams getting started.

What additional features do I get with Pro?

Pro plan includes unlimited projects, AI-powered task prioritization, team collaboration tools, advanced analytics dashboard, priority support, and all premium features.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade from Free to Pro or downgrade from Pro to Free at any time. Changes take effect immediately with prorated billing.

Is there a free trial for the Pro plan?

We offer a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan so you can experience all premium features before making a commitment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. All payments are processed securely.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your Pro subscription, contact us for a full refund.
